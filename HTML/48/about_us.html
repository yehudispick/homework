About Us

Johnny's is a Glatt Kosher meat restaurant established in 1998 serving Ozone Park, NY. Our menu consists of high quality and fresh ingredients and comprises of freshly made subs, meat sandwiches, chicken sandwiches, soups, salads, egg rolls, wraps, burgers, hot dogs, corn dogs, platters, burritos, tacos, french fries, platters, special sauces and a kids menu. Come in today and try it for yourself.
